CORE MANUFACTURING CAPABILITIES 核心製造能力

6061 Aluminum Machining 6061 鋁合金加工

High-speed 3, 4, and 5-axis milling of 6061-T6 aluminum. Ideal for structural components requiring high strength-to-weight ratios and excellent corrosion resistance. 6061-T6 鋁合金的高速 3、4、5 軸銑削。適用於要求高強度重量比和優異耐腐蝕性的結構件。

  • Structural Applications 結構性應用

440C Steel Processing 440C 鋼加工

Precision machining of hardened 440C stainless steel. Expertise in maintaining dimensional stability on high-hardness materials for wear-resistant applications. 硬化 440C 不鏽鋼的精密加工。擅長在高硬度材料上保持尺寸穩定性，用於耐磨應用。

  • Wear-Resistant Parts 耐磨零件加工
