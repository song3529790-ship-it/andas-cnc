Our Capabilities

Advanced CNC machining solutions for precision manufacturing

3-Axis CNC Machining

Our 3-axis machining centers deliver high-precision milling and drilling for standard components. Ideal for flat surfaces, pockets, and holes with excellent repeatability.

  • Tolerance: ±0.03mm
  • Max workpiece: 800 x 500 x 400mm
  • Batch size: 100-10,000 pcs
  • Surface finish: Ra 1.6-3.2

4-Axis CNC Machining

With rotary axis capability, we can machine complex geometries in a single setup. This reduces handling time and improves positional accuracy for multi-sided parts.

  • Tolerance: ±0.01mm
  • Rotary indexing: 0.001° resolution
  • Complex contours & angles
  • Reduced setup time
